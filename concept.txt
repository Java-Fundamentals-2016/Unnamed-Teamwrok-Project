#World
-Terrain - generated OR static
-Character
-Enemies
-Loot - visible or hidden
-Traps - visible or hidden
-Activated elements - switches, levers, atctivated doors or chests, etc.

#Entity
-Position
-Representation - appearance and animations
-Alive

#Main Character : Entity
-HP
-Attack power
-Armor
-Inventory - equipped items, money, etc.
-Modifiers - item effects, buffs, status effects

#Enemies : Entity
-HP
-Attack power
-Armor
-Modifiers - status effects
-Behaviour - basic AI
-Loot table - possible item, money and experience drops

#Loot : Entity
-Effect type - permanent (when equiped), one time (consumable), timed (buff)
-Effect - alter character stats, alter world (for keys)
-Narrative elements - text, decoration
